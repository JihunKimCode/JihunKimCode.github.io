Science Undergraduate Laboratory Internships

Oak Ridge National Laboratory

May 2023 - Jul 2023

I engaged in research under the guidance of my mentor, Junchuan Fan, aiming to enhance semantic interoperability among diverse geographic categorization schemes. Leveraging large language models, I explored natural language processing tools, particularly focusing on information retrieval and semantic search.

The research involved collecting data, which I analyzed and visualized using diverse graph types such as clustered bar graphs and box plots. Coordinating the research direction, interpreting results, and concluding were fascinating aspects of this experience, providing valuable insights into the intricacies of the research process.

Undergraduate Teaching Assisstant

University of Tennessee, Knoxville

Aug 2022 - May 2023

As an undergraduate teaching assistant at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, I supported Physics and Modeling for Engineers I & II courses. My responsibilities included setting up equipment for problem-solving involving physics principles.

Additionally, I played a key role in enhancing students' proficiency with commonly used programs like Excel and MATLAB. I contributed to deepening their understanding of fundamental physical concepts, including Vectors, Newton's Laws, Conservation of Energy, Mass Moment of Inertia, and Torque.

Through these efforts, I aimed to help students overcome challenges in physics, reducing resistance and establishing a solid foundation for their future engineering courses.
